Project Context

CodeNPlay is an educational platform designed to help young learners discover coding and digital skills in a fun, engaging way. During my internship at BubbleTech, I contributed to improving the website’s user experience and interface design, working closely with the team and following client feedback cycles.

My Role

  • Participated in client meetings and gathered feedback
  • Worked on UX thinking (navigation, structure, clarity)
  • Designed wireframes and interactive prototypes in Figma
  • Collaborated with developers to align design and implementation
  • Iterated on designs based on reviews and project constraints

UX Focus

My main focus was to keep the experience clear and reassuring for parents while maintaining an engaging tone for younger users. I paid attention to information hierarchy, readability, and consistency across key pages.

Final Outcome

The live website reflects collaborative improvements made during the internship.
